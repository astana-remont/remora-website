FAQ

What to Know Before You Start

Welcome to our Home Upgrade Journal, where we share simple tips and reliable advice.

How long does a home renovation take?

A home renovation timeline depends on the size and complexity of the project. Small updates may take 2–4 weeks, while larger or full-home renovations can take 6–12 weeks or more. After reviewing your project details, we provide a clear schedule.

Is the renovation process disruptive?

We carefully plan each stage to reduce noise, dust, and inconvenience while keeping your home functional and comfortable throughout the renovation period with proper safety measures in place.

Do you provide a fixed project timeline?

Yes, every project includes a detailed timeline based on scope and complexity, outlining key milestones, expected completion dates, and regular progress updates to ensure transparency from start to finish.

Are materials included in the renovation cost?

Most renovation packages include materials, and we clearly list all costs, brands, and quality options upfront so you know exactly what is included before the project begins.

Can I stay in my home during renovation?

In many situations, homeowners can remain in their homes during renovation, depending on work areas, safety requirements, project scale, and the level of disruption involved.
